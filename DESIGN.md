# Design System: Retro Hardware Vault
Theme: Cyber-Archeology / High-Fidelity Retro

## 1. Visual Identity
- **Vibe:** "The terminal at the end of the world." High contrast, scanlines, physical tactile feel in a digital space.
- **Palette:**
  - `Void Black`: #050505 (Background)
  - `Amber Glow`: #FFB000 (Primary/Accent)
  - `Phosphor Green`: #33FF00 (Success/Signal)
  - `Signal Red`: #FF2A00 (Error/Warning)
  - `Steel Gray`: #2A2A2A (Surface/Cards)

## 2. Typography
- **Headings:** `Space Mono` or `JetBrains Mono` (Technological, brutalist).
- **Body:** `Inter` (Clean, legible) or `Berkeley Mono` (if available).

## 3. Components (Shadcn/21st.dev Style)
- **Cards:** "Data Slates" - frosted glass with visible circuitry/grid patterns.
- **Buttons:** Physical "clicky" feel, intense hover glows.
- **Layout:** Grid-based, bento-box style for hardware specs.

## 4. Imagery (Nano Banana 2)
- **Hero:** 4K macro shot of a custom retro computer motherboard, amber lighting, cinematic depth of field.
- **Textures:** Subtle CRT scanline overlays, dust particles in light shafts.
